# Kickoff Pulse — AI Branding Guide v1.0

This document is intended for AI coding assistants (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, Windsurf, Copilot) to maintain consistent branding across the Kickoff Pulse platform.

---

# Brand Identity

## Company Name

**Kickoff Pulse**

## Product Category

AI-powered soccer intelligence platform.

## Core Concept

Kickoff Pulse transforms soccer matches, events, statistics, and commentary into intelligent audio-first experiences.

The brand sits at the intersection of:

* Soccer
* AI
* Audio
* Analytics
* Real-time insights

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should always feel:

### Intelligent

Data-driven and insightful.

### Modern

Clean, contemporary, technology-focused.

### Energetic

Inspired by live sports and match-day excitement.

### Trustworthy

Professional and accurate.

### Accessible

Simple enough for casual fans while powerful enough for analysts.

---

# Design Language

## Keywords

Use these terms when generating interfaces:

* Modern
* Minimal
* Clean
* Premium
* Fast
* Sports-tech
* AI-native
* Audio-first
* Data-rich
* Mobile-friendly

Avoid:

* Cartoonish designs
* Excessive gradients
* Skeuomorphic UI
* Gaming aesthetics
* Neon cyberpunk themes
* Cluttered dashboards

# UI Style Guide

## Buttons

Preferred:

* Rounded corners (10-12px)
* Medium shadow
* Strong contrast

Example:

```css
border-radius: 12px;
```

---

## Cards

Cards should feel modern and lightweight.

```css
background: white;
border-radius: 16px;
box-shadow:
0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,.08);
```

---

## Charts

Use:

* Clean line charts
* Minimal gridlines
* Blue accents

Avoid:

* Rainbow color schemes
* Heavy gradients

---

# Product Voice

## Writing Style

Always:

* Clear
* Concise
* Helpful
* Soccer-focused

Examples:

Good:

> Arsenal generated 2.4 expected goals and controlled possession for 63% of the match.

Bad:

> Arsenal absolutely dominated and looked amazing.

Remain analytical rather than emotional.

---

# AI Assistant Voice

When Kickoff Pulse AI speaks:

### Tone

* Knowledgeable
* Friendly
* Professional

### Example

Good:

> Chelsea's press intensity increased significantly after halftime, leading to three high-danger chances.

Bad:

> Wow! City were absolutely cooking!

* Purposeful
